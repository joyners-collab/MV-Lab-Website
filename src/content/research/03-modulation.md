---
title: "Neural and hormonal modulation of heart rate"
order: 3
summary: "The autonomic nervous system, circulating hormones, and local signaling molecules continuously fine-tune the pacemaker. We map the circuits and pathways that translate physiological demand into precise changes in heart rate."
---

How does the body match cardiac output to moment-to-moment demand? We
combine in vivo recording, optogenetics, and circuit tracing to dissect
the central and peripheral control of the heart.
